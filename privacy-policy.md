Privacy Policy – Offline Photo Text Copy

Last updated: 07/06/2026

This app ("Offline Photo Text Copy") does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Camera access: The app uses your device's camera to capture photos for the sole purpose of on-device text recognition (OCR). Photos are processed entirely locally on your device and are never saved, uploaded, or transmitted to any server.

Recognized text: Text extracted from photos is copied to your device's clipboard for you to use as you wish. This text is not stored by the app and is not transmitted anywhere.

No internet connection required: All processing happens offline, on your device.

No third-party sharing: We do not share any data with third parties, as we do not collect any data.

Changes to this policy: If this policy changes in the future (e.g. due to new features), this page will be updated accordingly.
